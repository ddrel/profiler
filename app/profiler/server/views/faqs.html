Frequently Asked Questions



What is The Profiler

The profiler is an skills assessment for IBMERs.


How to create an account?

1. Click "Create an account" button to register.

2. Fill in the Full Name textbox.

3. Enter your "IBM email" on Email textbox.

4. Create and confirm new password

What do I do after completing the questionnaire?

Click on the "Submit" button. After which, you will receive a Thank You note with the summary of your responses.

After I submit the questionnaire, can I still change my responses?

Once you have submitted the questionnaire, you can no longer edit it.

What if I was not able to complete the questionnaire, will my responses be saved?

Yes, once you go back to the questionnaire, you will be directed to the last question you have not answered yet.
